About Me

I'm a Web Developer seeking remote opportunities and freelance web projects. I'm experienced using HTML, CSS, Javascript, and frameworks such as jQuery, Bootstrap, and Google's Materialize. I'm also familiar with using the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, NodeJS) in Web Development workflows.
Though my chosen media may have changed throughout the years, I've had a passion for design throughout my entire life. I was diagnosed with Epilepsy at the age of 15, and soon learned that I also suffered from photosensitive seizures (the type where one must avoid flashing lights). My disability has inspired me to focus on design with Accessibility at the forefront. In addition to my technical knowledge, I've accumulated over a decade of experience working to ensure customer satisfaction as a Food Server, Restaurant Manager, and remote Customer Service Representative. I'm familiar with the installation and use of modern tools such as Slack and Zoom that are often utilized for collaboration amongst a team.
